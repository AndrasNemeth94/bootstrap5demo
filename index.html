Bronze Pack

Bronze Pack offers you the opportunity to be introduced to basic web development & design. You can enter the world of cutting-edge technologies and innovative ideas in just a short amount of time. It takes up four weeks, 80 hours total. You will be able to design and create your own static website with the usage of HTML5 and CSS3.

100$

Silver Pack

Silver Pack introduces the most required front-end frameworks in web development like Angular.js and React.js. After you finished your first four weeks of learning about HTML5 and CSS3, you will be teached to create interactive websites with the basics like user input management and dynamic content creation. The course takes 240 hours in total.

200$

Golden Pack

Silver Pack includes Bronze and Silver packages as well. After you finished your courses, Golden Pack provides you knowledge on database types, the ways of communicate with them from the front end, and you will also be able to write you own back-end in Express.js for e-commerce websites. The course takes 480 hours in total.

300$
